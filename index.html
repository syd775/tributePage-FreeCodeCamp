Kristen Schaal's Voice Tribute Page
Kristen Schaal and some character's she voiced. From left to right, Kristen Schaal, Mabel Pines, Sarah Lynn, and Louise Belcher.

Kristen Schaal is an American actress, comedian, and writer with a unique voice leading her to voice-act in many TV shows and movies of varying genres. This page is made in tribute of some of her most iconic voice acting roles.

Mabel Pines

  • Show: Gravity Falls
  • Runtime: 2012-2016
  • Quote: "Haha, nacho earrings, I'm hilarious!"

Sarah Lynn

  • Show: BoJack Horseman
  • Runtime: 2014-2020
  • Quote: "I swear you are like the only one I have left, I am this close to falling off the deep end. (laughs) I know I'm smiling right now, but the light inside me is dying..."

Louise Belcher

  • Show: Bob's Burgers
  • Runtime: 2011-Present
  • Quote: "Oh boy, poor old thing is too proud to say he's going deaf. (shouting) Just be deaf dad, it's fine, it's natural for your age."
